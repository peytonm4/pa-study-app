<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Docker Compose infra services</name>
  <files>docker-compose.yml</files>
  <action>
Create `docker-compose.yml` at repo root with two services:

**postgres:**
- Image: `postgres:16`
- Environment: `POSTGRES_DB=studyapp`, `POSTGRES_USER=studyapp`, `POSTGRES_PASSWORD=studyapp`
- Ports: `5432:5432`
- Volume: `postgres_data:/var/lib/postgresql/data`
- Healthcheck: `["CMD-SHELL", "pg_isready -U studyapp"]`, interval 5s, timeout 5s, retries 5

**minio:**
- Image: `quay.io/minio/minio:latest` (NOT `minio/minio` — Docker Hub images were deprecated Oct 2025)
- Command: `server /data --console-address ":9001"`
- Environment: `MINIO_ROOT_USER=minioadmin`, `MINIO_ROOT_PASSWORD=minioadmin`
- Ports: `9000:9000` (S3 API), `9001:9001` (web console)
- Volume: `minio_data:/data`

Named volumes section: `postgres_data:` and `minio_data:`

Do NOT add `depends_on` between services — they're independent.
  </action>

  </verify>
